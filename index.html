About The Party

Lal Hit Janta Party is a youth-focused movement against corruption, fake propaganda, political manipulation, and anti-development politics.


Our goal is to build a modern India based on discipline, transparency, technology, development, and public accountability.


We believe politics should serve people — not dynasties, influencers, or corruption networks.

Our Vision

Clean Politics

Remove corruption, fake promises, and criminal influence from politics.

Digital India

Transparent governance through technology and public accountability.

Youth Power

Give opportunities to educated and skilled youth leaders.

Development First

Infrastructure, jobs, education, safety, and national growth above all.

Party Rules & Regulations

No Corruption

Any proven corruption leads to immediate removal.

No Hate Politics

Religion and caste politics are strictly prohibited.

Public Accountability

Leaders must publish yearly work reports.

Nation First

Development and public welfare above personal power.
